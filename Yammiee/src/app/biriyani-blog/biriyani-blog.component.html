At Yammiee, our Chicken Biryani isn’t just a meal — it's a memory wrapped in layers of spice, aroma, and love. Our chefs bring generations of tradition to your plate, making every bite unforgettable.

🍗 The Heart of the Dish — The Chicken

We start with fresh, tender cuts of chicken marinated for hours in a blend of yogurt, turmeric, red chili, and garam masala. This ensures each piece soaks up all the rich flavors before it’s cooked to juicy perfection.

🍚 Basmati Rice — The Fragrant Canvas

Our long-grain basmati rice is steamed with saffron and whole spices like cardamom, cinnamon, and star anise. This creates the perfect base — aromatic, fluffy, and golden.

🌿 Secret Spice Blend

What sets Yammiee’s Biryani apart is our unique hand-ground spice mix. A family-kept secret, it strikes the perfect balance between heat, aroma, and flavor — elevating the entire dish.

🔥 Dum Cooking — The Traditional Way

We use the age-old dum method to seal in the flavors. Layers of marinated chicken and spiced rice are slow-cooked in a sealed pot, allowing the ingredients to cook in their own steam, resulting in a deeply flavorful dish.

🥗 Served With Love

Each order of Chicken Biryani is served hot with a side of cooling raita, tangy salad, and a piece of lemon. Whether you dine in or order online, we promise a royal meal every time.
