At Yammiee, we take you on a flavorful journey to Bengal with our Traditional Bengali Thali. A perfect harmony of spices, textures, and heritage — every dish tells a story of comfort, culture, and culinary excellence.

🐟 Shorshe Ilish — The Crown Jewel

We use the iconic Hilsa fish, marinated and cooked gently in a pungent mustard sauce with green chilies and mustard oil. It’s bold, flavorful, and the true pride of Bengali kitchens.

🦐 Chingri Malai Curry — Royal Indulgence

Succulent prawns are simmered in a smooth coconut milk gravy, enriched with aromatic spices and ghee. This creamy dish is a luxurious addition to the Thali, much loved by seafood lovers.

🥦 Shukto — The Bitter Beginning

A traditional starter, Shukto is a lightly bitter medley of vegetables balanced with sweetness and a touch of mustard and fenugreek. It cleanses the palate and awakens the appetite.

🍆 Begun Bhaja — Crispy Delight

Thick slices of eggplant, marinated in turmeric and salt, then shallow-fried in mustard oil until golden and crispy. A comforting and crunchy favorite.

🍨 Mishti Doi — The Sweet Finale

Our meal ends with the quintessential Bengali dessert — Mishti Doi. This sweetened, caramelized yogurt is served chilled, bringing a creamy, tangy end to your traditional feast.

🎉 Experience the Culture

Every bite in this Thali carries warmth and nostalgia, making it perfect for festive meals or a hearty family dinner. Crafted with love, served with tradition — that’s the Yammiee promise.
